Instructions

  1. Complete page 1 and page 2 on the first form. These details will replicate to the other 9 forms.
  2. Ensure you fill out all fields that apply.
  3. Enter a 4-digit postcode and click Generate Forms.
  4. Add participant photo in the photo box controls via the upload button.
  5. Review venue pages, then use Print / Save as PDF.
  6. CHECK ALL PREMISE NAME AND ADDRESSES ARE CORRECT.
  7. Take the printed form to the venue and ask for the licensee to sign.
  8. NOTE: If you take this to a chain of bottle shops or pubs you may be excluded from all the venues under the licensee/group.
